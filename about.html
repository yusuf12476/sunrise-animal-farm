Our Story

Established in 2010, Sunrise Farm began with a simple dream: to create a place where animals could thrive in a natural, loving environment. We believe in sustainable farming practices and the ethical treatment of all our animals. From the smallest chick to the largest bull, each creature is a part of our family and is cared for with the utmost respect and dedication.

Our commitment extends beyond our gates. We strive to be a positive force in our community, providing fresh, high-quality products and an educational experience for all who visit. We invite you to explore our website and learn more about what makes Sunrise Farm a special place.

Our Farming Philosophy

We believe that great food starts with a deep respect for nature. Our philosophy is built on three core principles.

Animal Welfare

Our animals are family. They live stress-free lives with open pastures to roam, fresh air to breathe, and natural diets. Happy animals lead to healthier, higher-quality products.

Soil Health

We feed the soil, and the soil feeds us. Through practices like rotational grazing and composting, we build rich, living soil that sequesters carbon and grows nutrient-dense food without synthetic inputs.

Biodiversity

A thriving farm is a diverse ecosystem. We cultivate wildflowers for pollinators, protect natural waterways, and encourage a balance of wildlife, creating a resilient and vibrant farm environment.

Our Journey Through the Years

2010

The Dream Begins

Sunrise Farm is established with a small plot of land, a handful of chickens, and a dream of sustainable farming.

2012

First Harvest

We celebrate our first successful harvest and begin selling fresh eggs and goat milk at the local farmers' market.

2015

Expanding the Family

The farm welcomes its first herd of cattle, expanding our offerings to include high-quality, grass-fed beef.

2022

Going Digital

Our online store opens, making it easier for customers nationwide to order fresh farm products for delivery.

2024

A Sweet Addition

We introduce our first apiary, welcoming honeybees to the farm and beginning our journey into sustainable beekeeping and delicious raw honey production.

Our Team & Commitment

Our Commitment

Our team is the heart and soul of Sunrise Farm. We are a group of passionate individuals—farmers, animal lovers, and nature enthusiasts—united by a shared commitment to ethical and sustainable agriculture. Every member of our team pours their dedication into the daily care of our animals and the land we steward. We believe that happy, well-cared-for animals and a healthy environment are the cornerstones of the high-quality, wholesome products we are proud to offer our community. It's not just a job for us; it's a way of life we are excited to share with you.
