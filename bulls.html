Our Bulls & Heifers

The cattle are the majestic backbone of Sunrise Farm. Our bulls and heifers enjoy a life of open pastures, grazing on lush, green grass. We are dedicated to raising strong, healthy animals through natural and humane practices.

Breeds on Our Farm:

  • Angus: Renowned for their hardiness and excellent maternal instincts, our Angus cattle are a source of pride. They are known for their solid black or red coats.
  • Hereford: With their distinctive white faces and red bodies, Herefords are a gentle and docile breed, making them a pleasure to work with.

Our herd is managed with a focus on low-stress handling techniques. They have constant access to fresh water and are rotationally grazed to ensure the health of both the animals and the land. This careful stewardship results in happy, thriving cattle.
