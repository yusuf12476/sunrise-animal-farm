Our Ducks

You'll often see our ducks waddling in a line on their way to the pond, their cheerful quacks echoing across the farm. They are excellent foragers and help with natural pest control, all while providing us with rich, delicious eggs perfect for baking.

Breeds on Our Farm:

  • Pekin: The classic white farm duck, Pekins are friendly, hardy, and great egg layers. Their bright orange bills and feet make them stand out.
  • Mallard: With the drakes' iconic iridescent green heads and the hens' mottled brown plumage, Mallards add a touch of wild beauty to our flock.

Our farm pond is the ducks' favorite spot, where they can swim, dive, and preen their feathers. We provide them with a safe, clean environment where they can exhibit their natural behaviors, ensuring they live happy and productive lives.
