Honey Glazed Lamb Chops

A quick and elegant meal, these lamb chops are seared to perfection and finished with a sweet and savory glaze featuring our farm's raw honey.

Prep Time

10 mins

Cook Time

10-15 mins

Servings

2-4

Ingredients

  • 8 Sunrise Farm Lamb Chops
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp fresh rosemary, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

For the Glaze:

  • 3 tbsp Sunrise Farm Raw Honey
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Instructions

  1. Prepare Lamb: Pat the lamb chops dry with a paper towel. Season generously with salt, pepper, minced garlic, and chopped rosemary.
  2. Sear the Chops: Heat olive oil in a large, heavy-bottomed skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is shimmering, carefully place the lamb chops in the pan. Sear for 3-4 minutes per side, until a golden-brown crust forms.
  3. Make the Glaze: While the chops are searing, whisk together the honey, soy sauce, and balsamic vinegar in a small bowl.
  4. Glaze and Finish: Reduce the heat to medium-low. Pour the glaze over the lamb chops in the skillet. Cook for another 1-2 minutes, turning the chops to coat them in the bubbling glaze.
  5. Rest and Serve: Remove the lamb chops from the skillet and let them rest on a cutting board for 5 minutes. This is crucial for juicy meat. Serve immediately, spooning any extra glaze from the pan over the top.

Serving Suggestion

These lamb chops pair beautifully with creamy mashed potatoes and roasted asparagus. The simple, elegant flavors make it a perfect dish for a special occasion.
